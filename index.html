Who We Are

A Focused Exporter
Built for Global Trade

Based in Bengaluru, India, Svarna Mercantile was founded with a singular purpose — to deliver reliable, high-quality auto parts to distributors and bulk buyers worldwide.

We specialise in a tightly curated catalog — not hundreds of parts, but three categories where we ensure every product meets the consistency and specification that global distributors demand.

Our model is built on trust, transparency, and long-term partnerships. We work exclusively with distributors and wholesale buyers — not retail consumers — which means your business gets dedicated attention and competitive pricing.
